About Us.

Balderdash Studios is the creation of Harvey Gilman a dedicated and hardworking Unreal Engine professional mentored by Edward Dawson-Taylor at CGPRO where he graduated becoming the youngest person ever to do so.

Hi! I'm Harvey,

All my life I wanted to be a game developer and in 2019 I started my game dev journey. Since then I have been developing my game Paradise, and have been working on various other projects and have even created short films.


I started off learning Blender a free and open source 3D modeling program that is used by game devs around the world.



Then I begain learning Unity a popular free* game engine used by many indie and AAA dev teams.
